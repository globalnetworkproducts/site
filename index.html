Welcome to Global Network Products (GNP) Ltd.

The launch of our exciting new business, took place on 2nd December 2020, and our website is now in the final stages of construction.

GNP is based in Swindon, Wiltshire, and it is our mission to provide outstanding customer service, combined with high quality, low cost items to the Telecoms, Data Network, and Construction Industries.

We are now open for business and able to supply Telecoms, Datacomms and Engineering Stores items, including Underground, Overhead and Site Equipment, Copper and Fibre Optic products, Tools, Testers, Internal Telecoms/ Data items, Personal Safety and Consumables.

The specific types of items which we are able to supply now and/or shortly includes:

  • Gas Detectors
  • Spades Rabbitting, Shovel Holer, Bars Digging and a full range of Shocksafe, Fibre Glass, Wooden and Steel handled tools - hundreds of individual items
  • Hand and Power Tools - Hammers, Nippers, Pliers and Cutters - Literally thousands of items
  • Ripley Tools Fibre & Copper Strippers, Cutters, Slitters, Crimpers and so on.
  • Ripley Fibre Testers - Power Meters, Light Sources, Visual Fault Locators, OTDR's and so on.

  • Consumables - Cable Ties, Tapes and Cable Fixings
  • Duct Brushes and Mandrels
  • Dropwire Clamps - Copper and Fibre
  • Xelan Data Cables, Modules, Patch Panels and Cabinets
  • TDR's Faultcaster, Lancaster and Quintest Testers
